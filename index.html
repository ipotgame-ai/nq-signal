What is VIX? The market's fear gauge. When it rises sharply, traders are scared and selling. When it drops, confidence returns.

Below 15 → Calm market, bullish for NQ.
15–25 → Normal range, mixed signals.
Above 25 → High fear, bearish for NQ.

When VIX data is unavailable (e.g. outside CBOE hours), the bot automatically switches to Mag 7 as the primary signal.

Why this matters: These 7 stocks make up ~30% of NASDAQ. When most move together, NQ follows.

When VIX is unavailable, 5+ stocks moving in the same direction is enough to trigger a signal.
