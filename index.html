VIX — Volatility Index

Tap to learn what this means ↓
What is VIX? Think of VIX as the market's "fear gauge." When it's low, traders are calm and confident — usually good for prices going up. When it spikes, traders are scared and selling.

Below 15 → Calm market. Bullish signal for NQ.
15 to 25 → Normal range. Mixed signals — be cautious.
Above 25 → Fear is high. Bearish signal for NQ.

Today's VIX directly affects how wide Claude sets your stop loss and take profit.
