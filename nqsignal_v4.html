VIX — Volatility Index

Tap to learn more ↓
What is VIX? The market's fear gauge. Rising VIX = fear = NQ typically falls. Falling VIX = confidence = NQ typically rises.

VIX falling/selling → Fear dropping = NQ goes UP ↑ LONG
VIX rising/buying → Fear rising = NQ goes DOWN ↓ SHORT

VIX <15 = low fear (bullish NQ) · VIX 15–25 = neutral · VIX >25 = high fear (bearish NQ)

When VIX data is unavailable, the bot automatically switches to Mag 7 as the primary signal.

Relative Strength Index (14-period)
What is RSI? RSI measures price momentum on a 0–100 scale.

Below 30 → Oversold, potential bounce (Bullish for NQ)  |  Above 70 → Overbought, potential pullback (Bearish)  |  40–60 → Neutral momentum

RSI confirms or contradicts VIX + Mag 7 signals for higher accuracy.
